asi-tinymce

This component is an integration of the TinyMCE, see : https://www.tinymce.com/ In your package.json add the dependency : "tinymce": "4.6.5" In your angular-cli.json add "../node_modules/tinymce/tinymce.js", "../node_modules/tinymce/themes/modern/theme.js", "../node_modules/tinymce/plugins/link/plugin.js", "../node_modules/tinymce/plugins/paste/plugin.js", "../node_modules/tinymce/plugins/table/plugin.js" "../node_modules/tinymce/plugins/textcolor/plugin.js", "../node_modules/tinymce/plugins/colorpicker/plugin.js",
